Request Account Deletion - Fealty

To request deletion of your Fealty account and associated data, please email us at:

privacy@fealty.app

Please include your username or email address in your request.

What data will be deleted?

  • User account information (username, email)
  • All claims and POI data
  • Leaderboard rankings
  • Profile information and statistics
  • All game progress and achievements

Data retention

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request. This action cannot be undone.
