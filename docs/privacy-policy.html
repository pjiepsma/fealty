Privacy Policy - Fealty

Last updated: December 1, 2024

1. Introduction

Fealty ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Username: Required for account creation and displayed in leaderboards
  • Email Address: Required for account creation and authentication
  • User ID: Unique identifier for your account

2.2 Location Data

  • Precise Location (GPS): Required for POI detection, claiming territories, and game functionality
  • Location data is collected continuously while the app is in use
  • Location data is stored in our database to track claims and rankings

2.3 App Activity

  • App Interactions: Claims, POI interactions, and game progress
  • Search History: Optional search queries in rankings (processed ephemerally)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To enable POI claiming, rankings, leaderboards, and game features
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, authenticate users
  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve game features

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely in our database (Supabase)
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. Your data is only used within the Fealty app for game functionality.

6. Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • You can request account deletion at any time
  • Upon deletion request, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

7. Your Rights

  • You can access your personal data through the app
  • You can request deletion of your account and data
  • To delete your account, visit: Delete Account

8. Children's Privacy

Fealty is designed for users aged 6 and above. We comply with Google Play Families Policy. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to request deletion.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please visit our Account Deletion page.
