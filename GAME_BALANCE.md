## ‚è±Ô∏è **Claim Duration Analysis**

### **Current: 5 Minutes Minimum**
**Pros:**
- ‚úÖ Strong differentiator from Turf's instant claims
- ‚úÖ Forces meaningful presence at locations
- ‚úÖ Prevents drive-by gaming
- ‚úÖ Creates real engagement time

**Cons:**
- ‚ùå Might feel too long for casual users
- ‚ùå Could be frustrating in bad weather
- ‚ùå May discourage quick exploration sessions
- ‚ùå High barrier to entry for new players

### **Alternative Duration Options:**

#### **Option A: 3 Minutes (More Accessible)**
- Faster onboarding
- Still requires presence (not instant)
- Less commitment per claim
- Better for casual play sessions

#### **Option B: 2 Minutes (Competitive)**
- Quick claims for competitive play
- Still anti-drive-by (can't claim while moving)
- Allows more locations per session
- Lower time commitment

#### **Option C: Progressive System**
- Start with 2 minutes for new players
- Increase to 5 minutes for "premium" claims
- Unlock longer durations as rewards
- Balances accessibility with depth

#### **Option D: Location-Based Duration**
- Parks/Museums: 5 minutes (exploration time)
- Restaurants/Shops: 3 minutes (visit time)
- Monuments: 2 minutes (quick stops)
- Adapts to location type

### **My Recommendation:**
**Start with 3 minutes.** It's still meaningful presence but more accessible. You can always increase it later based on user feedback.
